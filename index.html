HOW TO SURVIVE

You are the fruit of the garden — apple, pear, peach, plum, orange; choose in Settings.
For a thousand years the serpent has fed on your kind, row by row, in the dark of the orchard.
It has already eaten everyone you ever knew. Tonight it finishes the harvest.

Gather what remains of your kin to keep your lantern burning.
When the light dies, it will already be behind you.
When the sound dies, it is closer than that.

When it closes in, press E to cast a gathered kin into the dirt.
The cobra will stop to feed. Your kin will feel it. Run anyway.
